Murder At The House

In July 2023 I published my first game on steam - Murder At The House. I designed all the graphics myself and programmed it entirely in C# using Unity.

Murder At The House is an indie, social-deducation, pixel, murder mystery game. 11 people are trapped in an old abandoned house together, 8 are innocent, one is a detective and the other is the murderer. You, either the detective or an innocent, must interrogate all the suspects and find clues littered around the house till you think you know who the murderer is. However, if you take too long, more innocents may die..
